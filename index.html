Our Treatments

Full Body treatment

Full body massage performed to energize the body and help improve overall health. Light and medium touch according to the pressure of your choice for relaxing mind states and letting go.

Half Body treatment

A half-body massage focusing on the upper body typically includes the head, neck, shoulders, back, arms, and hands. This can be beneficial for individuals experiencing upper body tension or discomfort.

Hands and Feet

Hands and Feet Treatments begin with a bath of warm water to refresh ,relax the client. They are done with a focus on the pressure points in the hands,feet and give relief to sore hands and tired feet allowing blood circulation to improve throughout the body.

Skincare

The increased circulation that comes with facial massages has another benefit: It stimulates collagen production and cell growth. Collagen production, which slows down during the aging process, is key to keeping skin elastic and preventing wrinkles.
